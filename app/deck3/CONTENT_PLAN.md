# Deck 3 — Statement of Qualifications

## Editorial direction

- Primary audience: environmental consultants and engineering firms.
- Format: crisp, concise, client-facing brochure; target 18–22 pages, with a hard ceiling of 30 pages only when qualification material genuinely requires it.
- Brand architecture: every reference must use `PDS (A Summit Drilling company)`.
- Contact and pricing: use Summit Drilling's unified contact details.
- Evidence: use currently published information, but avoid prominent counter-style statistics until the combined figures are formally confirmed.
- Production: US Letter portrait, fixed page dimensions, explicit page breaks, static print-safe layouts, and no hover-dependent content or animation.

## Recommended core document

1. **Cover** — Statement of Qualifications; Summit Drilling with PDS (A Summit Drilling company).
2. **Contents** — Short, clearly grouped index.
3. **One Company, Expanded Reach** — Acquisition story and the value of the combined platform.
4. **Who We Are** — Customer promise, mission, values, and what clients can expect.
5. **Where We Work** — East Coast, Southeast, and Gulf Coast coverage with locations map.
6. **Integrated Capabilities** — Investigation through remediation and site closure.
7. **Environmental & Geotechnical Drilling** — Core applications and project support.
8. **Sonic Drilling** — Capability, applications, and combined operational depth.
9. **Direct Push, Auger, Rotary & Coring** — Method selection and applications.
10. **Remediation & Injection Services** — In-situ treatment and field implementation.
11. **Environmental Construction & Vacuum Excavation** — Systems, excavation, soft-dig, and support services.
12. **Geophysics & Utility Locating** — Integrated pre-drilling investigation and risk reduction.
13. **PFAS Experience** — Sampling, drilling, decontamination protocols, and sensitive-site execution.
14. **Fleet & Operational Capacity** — Equipment categories and deployment capability without unverified counters.
15. **Health, Safety & Quality** — Programs, training, field leadership, and aligned safety culture.
16. **Licenses & Industries Served** — Regional qualifications and relevant end markets.
17. **Representative Experience** — Three or four concise project profiles focused on challenge, approach, and outcome.
18. **Let's Get to Work** — Summit unified sales contact, phone, email, web address, and closing message.

## Optional qualification modules

Add only when needed for a specific pursuit or procurement package:

- Detailed equipment and rig matrix
- State licenses and certifications
- Insurance documentation
- Safety statistics and supporting records
- Additional project sheets
- Key staff qualifications

## Source hierarchy

1. Summit Drilling website and acquisition announcement
2. PDS website and current PDS SOQ
3. Confirmed internal data supplied during review

Published figures may be used in body copy where helpful, but the layout should not depend on large numeric counters.
